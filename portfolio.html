Smart bus interchange

A small prototype created using raspberry pi and audiuno to mimick a smart bus interchange.

It was done as one of my module's graded assignment where I had partnered up with my classmates to achieve this prototype.

It is a prototype to help monitor humidity and temperature, monitor the weather to deploy shelter and detect crashes or vehicles.

The data from the sensors were then uploaded to a platform service called ThingSpeak. The platform allows you can send data, create instant visualization of live data and send alerts to users.
